This platform consolidates over 12 months of analytical development into a unified intelligence system, enabling leadership to:

# **2\. Business Problem Statement**

## **2.1 Revenue Attribution Complexity**

Following regulatory changes in May 2025, ASO now operates within a dual-channel monetization structure:

* Apple In-App Purchase (IAP)  
* External payment gateways

This shift introduced ambiguity in:

* Total revenue reporting  
* Platform-level profitability  
* Regulatory compliance tracking

## **2.2 Labor Inefficiency**

Operational analysis revealed a significant imbalance:

* 50% of total sales occur within only 20 hours per week  
* Staffing remains evenly distributed across all hours

This results in:

* Lost revenue during peak demand  
* Excess labor cost during low-demand periods  
* Lack of quantifiable optimization strategy

## **2.3 Algorithmic Volatility**

An algorithm update on April 26, 2026 (“Keyword Earthquake”) caused:

* 16,000+ keyword ranking losses  
* 40% drop in discovery traffic  
* Shift toward AI-driven discovery (AEO vs. traditional SEO)

This created a gap in:

* Impact measurement  
* Strategic prioritization  
* Recovery tracking

# **3\. Dashboard Solution Architecture**

The dashboard is structured into three interconnected analytical narratives, each aligned to a specific business function and decision-making objective.

## **3.1 Revenue & Monetization Intelligence**

**Objective:** Ensure accurate revenue tracking across platforms

**Key Insights:**

* Total Net Revenue: $7.31M (April 2026\)  
* Platform Split: 88.1% Apple IAP / 11.9% External  
* Redownload Ratio: 1.97 (strong user retention)  
* Identified 40% revenue decline on April 26

**Outcome:**  
Enables Finance to validate revenue integrity, track regulatory impact, and support forecasting.

## **3.2 Labor Optimization & Peak Hour Scheduling**

**Objective:** Align staffing with revenue demand

**Key Insights:**

* Peak SPLH: $387 per FTE-hour  
* Non-Peak SPLH: $287 per FTE-hour  
* Efficiency Gain: 35% higher during peak hours  
* Peak window: 5 PM – 9 PM daily

**Business Impact:**

* Potential revenue lift: $20M–$25M annually  
* Reduced burnout: 58,400 FTE-hours annually

**Outcome:**  
Provides a quantified, data-backed staffing optimization strategy.

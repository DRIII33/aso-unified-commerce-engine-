## Synthetic Data Generation

### Purpose

Synthetic data was generated to simulate realistic operational scenarios for demonstration purposes, allowing the portfolio creator to showcase:
- End-to-end data pipeline architecture
- SQL query optimization (BigQuery and Snowflake dialects)
- Python data engineering skills
- Business metrics framework design
- Analytics Development Lifecycle (ADLC) execution

### Modeling Approach

**All synthetic data is randomly generated** based on publicly documented market parameters:

| Parameter | Source | Basis |
|-----------|--------|-------|
| Transaction Volume | Industry benchmarks | App Store daily transaction scale (estimated public range) |
| Transaction Amounts | Apple Pricing | Publicly listed App Store pricing ($0.99-$149.99) |
| Redownload Ratio | Industry research | 2:1 ratio documented in retail/app market studies |
| External Payment Adoption | Regulatory filing | 15% adoption modeled from May 2025 ruling implications |
| Peak Hour Concentration | NRF Research | 50% of sales in 20% of hours (published retail standard) |
| Algorithmic Traffic Impact | Published reports | 40% decline modeled from documented April 26, 2026 industry event |
| Labor Headcount | Industry norms | 20-50 FTE typical for regional operations center (public benchmarks) |
